Report Animal Neglect and Cruelty

Please use the following form to share as much as you can about the animal abuse or neglect situation. We care deeply about all animals and are eager to help.

Provide a detailed report to help our Humane Officer appropriately address your case.

Once you report animal abuse, a humane officer will be notified and assess the situation, and may bring the case to court.

Please be aware that misreporting can have serious consequences for the shelter, humane officers and animals involved.

Any shelter must be proportional to the animal size, have at least three walls, a waterproof roof, and protects the animal for regional from the elements.

Sanitation: Animals must be in a space free of excretions and other waste materials, dirt and trash that may be a health hazard.
Food & Water: Animals need access to portable drinking water and edible food.

Animals may be left outside if the following conditions are met:
  • - Animal is not outside for more than half an hour when the temperature is below 32 F or above 90 F.
  • - The animal has access to portable drinking water, edible food, and adequate shade or shelter.
