Report Animal Neglect and Cruelty

Please use the following form to share as much as you can about the animal abuse or neglect situation. We care deeply about all animals and are eager to help.

Once you report animal abuse, a humane officer will be notified and assess the situation, and may bring the case to court.

Please be aware that misreporting can have serious consequences for the shelter, humane officers and animals involved.
