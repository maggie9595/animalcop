Sanitation: Animals must be in a space free of excretions and other waste materials, dirt and trash that may be a health hazard.
Food & Water: Animals need access to portable drinking water and edible food.

Report ▶

Animals may be left outside if the following conditions are met:
  • - Animal is not outside for more than half an hour when the temperature is below 32 F or above 90 F.
  • - The animal has access to portable drinking water, edible food, and adequate shade or shelter.
