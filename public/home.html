Report Animal Neglect and Cruelty

How to report animal abuse or neglect.

Animal cruelty, dog fighting and animal neglect are issues that are all too common in our area. The reality of these activities touches the lives of all citizens, pet-owning and non-pet-owning alike. At the Animal Rescue League, our Humane Officer provides investigative and law enforcement support to all of Allegheny County.


Read over the information below before reporting your case.

1. Only report if you are in Allegheny County

If you are reporting a case that occurred in a different county, use the Humane Society Police Officer Registry or call your local police office to find someone who has the required legal authority to respond.

2. Only report if you are a firsthand witness

Hearsay and secondhand information cannot be investigated and only those with firsthand knowledge of the abuse should file a report.

3. Do not report to multiple agencies

If you witness cruelty that is occurring in another county, refer to the Humane Society Police Officer Registry maintained by the Pennsylvania Department of Agriculture to find an officer who has the required legal authority to respond. If your county does not have a Humane Police Officer, contact your local police or District Attorney's Office.
